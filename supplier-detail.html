Business Overview

ABC Textiles Ltd. is a leading ready-made garment manufacturer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, with over 15 years of experience in producing high-quality apparel for global brands. We specialize in men's and women's casual wear, workwear, and fashion garments with a strong commitment to sustainability and ethical manufacturing.

Our state-of-the-art facility spans 50,000 square meters and employs over 500 skilled workers. We maintain strict quality control standards and hold multiple international certifications including ISO 9001, WRAP, and OEKO-TEX. Our production capacity allows us to handle orders from 500 to 50,000 units per style with flexible lead times.
