‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does verification take? +
Verification typically takes 12-24 hours for standard documents. Complex cases may take up to 3 business days.
Can I edit my products after uploading? +
Yes, you can edit product details, prices, and images anytime from your catalog management page.
What file formats are supported for documents? +
We accept PDF, JPG, PNG files up to 10MB each. Documents should be clear and readable.
How do I update my company information? +
Go to your Profile page and click "Edit Profile" to update company details, contact information, and business categories.
